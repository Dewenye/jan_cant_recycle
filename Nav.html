Importance of Recycling!

Many people not know this but not knowing the difference between which items are considered recyclable and what goes in the garbage bin has a big impact on the earth and us as well.

Recyclable Items Include:

Metals: Tins, Allimunum, Steel Cans
Paper/Cardbord: Cereal/Snack boxes, Phonebooks, magazines, mail, Office Paper
Glass: Food Containers/Jars, Soft Drinks and Beer/Wine/Liqour Bottles/

Non Recyclable Items are:

Loose Plastic Bags: Plastic Shooping bags and Plstic Strecth Wrap
Foam (polystyrene) Cups or Containers: Egg Cartons, Take out Trays/Containers, and Drinking Cups
Soiled Food Items: Paper Bags, Pizza Boxes, Soiled Paper Napkins/Tissue
Other: Broken or sharp Glass, Fast food packaging, and Plastic Utensils
